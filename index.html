Robot Design

Our robot has an intake system, conveyor belt and flywheels.

INTAKE: Our intake uses extended rubber wheels from GoBilda.

CONVEYOR + RAMP: Our ramp uses a conveyor system to bring the balls up the ramp.

FLYWHEELS: Our flywheels are 6000 rpm GoBilda motors to launch the ball into the goal easily.
